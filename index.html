LangSLAM: Language-Driven Semantic Gaussian Splatting SLAM

Sude Zhang1*, Zhiyong Zhang1*,
1Sun Yat-sen University
Paper Coming Soon

Abstract

Experiments performed on synthetic and real-world datasets demonstrate that our method achieves competitive tracking and mapping performance while showing excellent capabilities in 3D dense semantic mapping compared with closed-set semantic SLAM. Furthermore, our method exhibits its capabilities of open-set 3D scene reconstruction and language localization, demonstrating its robustness and flexibility in handling a wide range of semantic categories.
